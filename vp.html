Professional Vessel Polishing

Premium Finish

Restore the Shine and Protect Your Vessel

Sun, salt, and environmental factors quickly take a toll on a boat's gelcoat, leaving it dull, oxidized, and porous. Our rofessional Polishing service isn't just about looks—it's about restoring the structural integrity of your vessel's surface and providing a durable barrier against the elements.

We use a multi-stage process that includes deep cleaning, oxidation removal, machine buffing with premium marine compounds, and a final application of high-grade wax or ceramic sealants. This treatment not only brings back that "showroom shine" but also makes future cleaning much easier as dirt and salt struggle to bond with the polished surface.

Yes, our multi-stage buffing process is specifically designed to cut through heavy oxidation and remove the yellowing (tannin stains) often found on the waterline, restoring the original white or colored finish of your boat.

Depending on the exposure to the sun and maintenance habits, a high-quality wax finish usually lasts 6 to 12 months. For even longer protection, we recommend our ceramic coating options which can last multiple seasons.
