Preparing the Vessel for the Season

Don't let technical issues ruin your summer. Our "Season Prep" service is a complete turnkey solution that ensures your vessel is safe, efficient, and looking its best before it hits the water.

We handle everything from the application of high-quality antifouling protection to the replacement of sacrificial zinc anodes. Beyond the hull, we conduct a rigorous inspection of all onboard systems, including bilge pumps, navigation lights, and safety equipment, so you can enjoy the Adriatic Sea with total peace of mind.

Antifouling prevents marine organisms (algae, barnacles) from attaching to your hull. This not only protects the boat's material but also ensures better speed and significantly reduces fuel consumption.

We perform a Full System Check: this includes battery health, electrical connections, water pumps, steering systems, and all mandatory safety gear (life jackets, flares, fire extinguishers).

While we focus on the preparation, we can coordinate with local marinas for the crane service and ensure a final leak check once the boat is back in the water.
