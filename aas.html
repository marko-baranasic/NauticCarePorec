Reliable Assistance at Sea

24/7 Support

Fast and Professional Maritime Assistance - we dont offer towing at the moment

The sea is unpredictable, and even the best-prepared skippers can face challenges. Our Assistance at Sea service is designed to provide quick, professional support when you need it most. Whether it's a technical failure, a stuck anchor, or the need for a tow, Nautic Care Poreč is your reliable partner on the water.

We operate in the Poreč archipelago and surrounding waters, offering expert help for various maritime issues. From simple battery jump-starts to assisting with fouled propellers or providing support during difficult anchoring maneuvers in bad weather, we are just a call away to ensure you and your crew reach the shore safely.

First, ensure the safety of all passengers and drop anchor if possible to prevent drifting. Contact us immediately with your GPS coordinates and a description of the problem. We will head to your location as quickly as possible.
