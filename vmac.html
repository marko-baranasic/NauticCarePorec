Vessel Maintenance and Care

We ensure your boat stays in pristine condition through all seasons. Our comprehensive maintenance service is designed for owners who value longevity, aesthetics, and safety.

Using only premium marine-grade products, we provide thorough external washing, meticulous hull cleaning, and detailed technical inspections. Our goal is to catch potential issues before they become costly repairs, keeping your investment protected and ready for the sea at any moment.

Our standard package includes regular exterior washing, removal of salt and algae, hull cleaning, and a complete check of ropes, fenders, and deck equipment for any signs of wear or damage.

For vessels in the water year-round, we recommend at least bi-weekly washing and a monthly underwater hull inspection. This prevents stubborn marine growth and protects the gelcoat from UV and salt damage.

Yes, we offer professional machine polishing as part of our premium care to restore shine and apply a protective UV layer to your vessel's gelcoat.
