Professional Surveillance in Your Absence

Your vessel is a valuable investment that requires attention even when you are miles away. Our surveillance service provides complete peace of mind, knowing that a professional team is physically checking your boat on a regular basis.

We don't just look from the dock; we board the vessel to check for humidity, ensure proper ventilation, and verify that all bilge pumps and electrical systems are functioning. During the winter months or periods of bad weather, we perform extra inspections to ensure moorings are secure and the boat is safe from the elements. After every check, we can provide you with a photo or video report.

A standard check includes mooring tension inspection, checking the battery status, ventilating the interior to prevent mold/odors, and checking for any water ingress in the bilge areas.

Yes, upon request, we perform monthly engine startups to ensure all mechanical parts remain lubricated and the cooling system is operational, preventing "seizing" during long periods of inactivity.

We offer weekly or bi-weekly schedules, but we always perform unscheduled emergency visits immediately following heavy storms or strong winds (Bura/Jugo).
