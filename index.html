TIGER MARINE PROLINE 620 w Honda 150 VTEC Outboard motor

The Tiger Marine Proline 620 is designed for comfortable and safe day trips on the sea. With a length of 6.25 m and space for up to 10 people, it offers excellent stability, smooth handling, and plenty of room to relax, making it ideal for families and groups of friends.

The Honda 150 VTEC outboard engine combines strong performance with fuel efficiency. Delivering 140 hp (104.4 kW), it provides fast acceleration, quiet operation, and reliable power, while maintaining a low fuel consumption of only 13 liters per hour.

The boat is equipped with a sun canopy (bimini), an external shower, an audio system with external speakers, and a GPS chart plotter. These features ensure comfort, entertainment, and easy navigation during every trip.
